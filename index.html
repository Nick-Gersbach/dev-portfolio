About Me

I created my first website in 2011 when I discovered the world of blogging and wordpress. As time I went on I became curious about how websites are created from scratch and I taught myself HTML and CSS. From there I learned the fundamentals of Javascript and PHP and got to a point where I feel confident in turning any design into a fully functioning site.

When I'm not building websites I'm probably lifting weights, playing with my son, or grilling steaks.
